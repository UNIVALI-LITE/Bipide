Bitwise Operations

The binary logical or bitwise operation is the basis of all the computational calculation. In fact, these are the most basic operations that constitute the whole power of computers. Any operation, however complex it may seem, is translated internally by the processor for these operations.

            

The following table shows the operator of each bitwise operation C available at:

Operator Description
~ bitwise NOT
& bitwise AND
^ bitwise XOR
| bitwise OR
<< bitwise left shift
>> bitwise right shift
